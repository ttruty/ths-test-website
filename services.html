Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I'm the right client for you?

Are you a recruiter that has billed over $200K and loses sleep thinking: How am I going to grow my business? My referral network dried up, what do I do? I don't know what activities I should do each week to win business. If the answer is yes, you are exactly who I help.

How long does it take to get results?

I picked the name The Honest Salesperson because I was tired of people on LinkedIn making fake guarantees. While I do not guarantee overnight success, my 1-on-1 clients have seen $50,000+ revenue increases within their first 90 days of working together.

How long does a 1-on-1 engagement last?

Most of my engagements last 6 months, but my minimum commitment is for at least 3 months. My ultimate goal is always to be replaced—my job is to position you and your company to operate successfully without my guidance.

How do we start the relationship?

It starts with a conversation. Fill out the inquiry form or book a discovery call. If I feel that I can be of help, we will map out the exact next steps. If we aren't a fit, I will tell you that honestly.
